Winnie the Pooh Quiz App

Created with React Native

The Winnie the Pooh Quiz App is a delightful mobile application built with React Native, designed to test your knowledge of the charming world of Winnie the Pooh and his friends from the Hundred Acre Wood. Featuring an engaging and user-friendly interface, the app offers a variety of fun and interactive quizzes that challenge fans of all ages.

Project Screens

Design Concept

The design of Trina's Winnie the Pooh Quiz app focuses on delivering a visually appealing and user-friendly interface that reflects the whimsical and nostalgic charm of the Winnie the Pooh universe. The layout is clean and intuitive, ensuring that users can easily navigate through the quiz. High-quality images of iconic characters such as Pooh, Piglet, Tigger, and Eeyore are prominently featured, creating a visually engaging experience and making the quiz feel connected to its theme.

The color palette is light and cheerful, evoking a sense of playfulness that aligns with the characters' personalities. Elements such as dropdown menus for answers and a large, accessible "Submit Answers" button are designed to prioritize simplicity and ease of use, ensuring that users of all ages can participate effortlessly. The feedback modal pop-up is styled to seamlessly integrate with the overall aesthetic, offering a polished and cohesive user experience.

About the Winnie the Pooh Quiz

The Winnie the Pooh Quiz App is a delightful and interactive platform designed for fans of the beloved characters from the Hundred Acre Wood. The app allows users to test their knowledge of Winnie the Pooh, Piglet, Tigger, Eeyore, and other iconic characters through a series of engaging questions. Each question is paired with vibrant character images, bringing the whimsical world of Winnie the Pooh to life. Whether you’re a lifelong fan or new to these cherished characters, the app offers a fun and nostalgic way to connect with the classic stories.

Key Features

  • Interactive Quiz Interface: Engaging and user-friendly interface with quiz questions accompanied by character images.
  • Multiple Choice Answers: Use dropdown menus for each question, allowing users to select their answers with ease.
  • Dynamic Feedback: Provide real-time feedback through a modal pop-up, displaying the number of correct answers after submission.
  • Scoring System: Automatically calculate and display the total number of correct answers for user evaluation.
  • Thematic Branding: Include a personalized header and design elements aligned with the Winnie the Pooh theme.
  • Responsive Design: Optimize the layout for mobile screens, ensuring seamless functionality across devices.
  • Submit Button: A clearly labeled button for users to submit their answers, making interaction intuitive and accessible.
