Pokemon App

The Pokémon Card Manager App is a versatile mobile application that simplifies managing your Pokémon card collection. From organizing cards by type to editing details and displaying dynamic images, the app offers an intuitive interface and user-friendly features for a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Project Screens


Design Concept

The Pokémon App design emphasizes simplicity, vibrant visuals, and organization, inspired by the playful and nostalgic Pokémon universe. The app features a soft pastel background that provides a clean and calming environment, while vibrant colors like purple, red, and blue highlight interactive elements such as buttons and Pokémon types.

Pokémon cards are organized by type, with colorful headers and type-specific icons (e.g., water droplet, flame) that make browsing intuitive and visually appealing. Each card showcases the Pokémon’s name, card number, and dynamic image, with a focus on clarity and accessibility.

About the Pokemon App

The Pokémon App is a fun and interactive platform for Pokémon enthusiasts to manage their card collections effortlessly. It allows users to browse Pokémon cards organized by type, add new cards, edit existing ones, and delete unwanted cards.

The app is designed to provide an intuitive and visually engaging experience, with features like real-time updates and dynamic image support. Whether you're organizing your collection or expanding it with new Pokémon, this app offers all the tools you need to keep your cards organized and accessible. It’s the perfect companion for Pokémon card collectors and fans alike.

Key Features

  • Home Screen: Browse Pokémon cards neatly organized by type (Water, Fire, Grass, etc.), with vibrant headers and type-specific icons.
  • Add Pokémon: Easily add new cards by providing the Pokémon’s name, card number, and type through a simple form.
  • Edit Cards: Modify existing Pokémon details, such as name and card number, directly from the edit screen.
  • Delete Cards: Remove unwanted Pokémon cards with a confirmation dialog to prevent accidental deletions.
  • Dynamic Images: Manage card images via URLs, ensuring seamless updates and scalability.
  • Instant Updates: All changes, including additions and edits, are instantly reflected on the home screen for an up-to-date collection view.
  • User-Friendly Design: Clean layout, responsive design, and vibrant visuals ensure a delightful user experience.
