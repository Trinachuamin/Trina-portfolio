Pokemon App

The Pokémon Card Manager App is a versatile mobile application that simplifies managing your Pokémon card collection. From organizing cards by type to editing details and displaying dynamic images, the app offers an intuitive interface and user-friendly features for a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Project Screens


About Pokemon App

  • Home Screen: Explore Pokémon cards neatly organized by type, including Water, Fire, Grass, and Rock.
  • Edit Cards: Easily update Pokémon names and card numbers directly from the edit screen.
  • Delete Cards: Remove unwanted Pokémon cards with a simple delete action, complete with confirmation to prevent accidental deletions.
  • Add New Cards: Effortlessly expand your collection by adding new cards with the Pokémon’s name, card number, and type.
  • Dynamic Images: Leverage URL-based image management for real-time updates and seamless scalability.
  • Instant Updates: All edits and additions are instantly reflected on the home screen, ensuring a consistently organized and up-to-date collection view.

This app transforms Pokémon card management into an enjoyable, efficient, and visually engaging experience.
