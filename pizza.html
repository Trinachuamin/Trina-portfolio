PIZZA MENU APPLICATION

Created by Trina Chua

Design Concept:

For the pizza menu, all the pizzas are categorized and listed in a visually appealing grid layout. The categories include vegetarian pizzas, meat-lover pizzas, and specialty pizzas. Each card includes high-quality images, descriptions, and prices for each pizza.

Breadcrumb navigation is implemented at the top of the menu pages to help users know exactly where they are within the website. Each pizza category page displays 6 to 12 products, with their prices and descriptions styled consistently to maintain a polished and modern look.
