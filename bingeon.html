BingeOn - A Streaming App Design

Created using Figma

BingeOn is a user-centric platform for streaming movies and shows. This project focused on creating a visually appealing interface, improving user experience with seamless navigation, and enhancing overall engagement.

To provide users with a seamless streaming experience, allowing them to discover, watch, and manage a variety of movies, series, and exclusive content.

Project Screens

About BingeOn App Design

The BingeOn App is a sleek and immersive platform designed to revolutionize how users explore, manage, and enjoy their favorite movies and shows. With its intuitive layout and powerful features, BingeOn makes content discovery seamless and engaging. The app allows users to browse trending collections, manage personal watchlists, download content for offline viewing, and access detailed movie and show information.

The appâ€™s dynamic design features horizontal and vertical scrolling layouts, making navigation effortless and visually appealing. Advanced functionalities like conditional triggers, smooth animations, and smart transitions ensure a modern and responsive user experience. Notifications keep users updated on new arrivals, popular content, and shows leaving the platform soon. Personalized user profiles add a touch of customization, while the integrated video player offers customizable playback options for an enhanced streaming experience.

BingeOn combines a futuristic dark theme with neon green accents, clean typography, and intuitive icons to deliver a visually striking and functional app, making it the ultimate entertainment hub for movie and show enthusiasts.

Design Concept

  • Color Scheme: A sleek dark theme with neon green accents for a modern and immersive look. The high contrast makes content and icons stand out for better usability.
  • Typography: Minimalistic fonts with a tech-inspired aesthetic for readability and style. Bold headings and larger titles emphasize key sections.
  • Icons: Clean and intuitive icons for navigation and interactivity, such as download, watchlist, and share. Neon green outlines add a cohesive and futuristic design touch.
  • Navigation: A fixed bottom navigation bar ensures seamless access to Home, Search, Downloads, Watchlist, and Profile, providing intuitive navigation throughout the app.

User Flow & Interactions

  • Home Screen: A visually immersive home screen showcasing trending content, categorized by Series, Movies, Action, Anime, and more. Includes sections for "Continue Watching" and "New on BingeOn."
  • Search: A powerful search functionality with a responsive search bar to filter content by title, actor, or genre. Includes genre shortcuts (e.g., Drama, Comedy) for quick navigation.
  • Watchlist: Easily manage a personal watchlist with options to add or remove titles. Includes filters for categories like Series, Movies, and Action to organize content efficiently.
  • Content Details: Detailed content pages featuring trailers, synopsis, genre tags, and options to like, share, or download content for offline viewing.
  • Downloads: Download movies and shows with a user-friendly manager to track downloaded content, set storage limits, and enable auto-download for selected titles.
  • Profiles: Personalized user profiles with avatars and custom watch histories. Switch profiles easily and manage content preferences for each user.
  • Notifications: Get real-time updates on new arrivals, leaving soon, and popular content. Notifications are categorized for easy access.
  • Player Controls: Smooth video playback with customizable subtitles, playback speed adjustments, and casting options for smart devices.
