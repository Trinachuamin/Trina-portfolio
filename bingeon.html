BingeOn - A Streaming App Design

BingeOn is a user-centric platform for streaming movies and shows. This project focused on creating a visually appealing interface, improving user experience with seamless navigation, and enhancing overall engagement.

To provide users with a seamless streaming experience, allowing them to discover, watch, and manage a variety of movies, series, and exclusive content.

Project Screens

Features & Highlights

  • Profiles: Personalized user profiles with avatars.
  • Notifications: Highlight new arrivals, leaving soon, and popular updates.
  • Watchlist: Easy-to-manage personal watchlist for favorites.
  • Content Discovery: Categories for Series, Movies, Genres, and more.
  • Search & Recommendations: Search by title or genre, with suggestions for popular content.
  • Downloads: Enable offline viewing with a custom download manager.

UI Elements & Visual Design

Color Scheme: Dark theme with neon green accents for a modern and immersive feel.

Typography: Minimalistic fonts for better readability and aesthetics.

Icons: Clean, intuitive icons for navigation and interactivity.

User Flow & Interactions

Home Screen: Browse content by categories.

Profile Screen: Manage user profiles.

Search Screen: Search and filter content easily.

Downloads: Manage offline content efficiently.
