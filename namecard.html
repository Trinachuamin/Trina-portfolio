Name Card Design

A minimalistic and professional name card design that emphasizes easy readability and organized contact details.

Concept Sketches


Mood Board



The mood board showcases a refined color palette of taupe, blush, and neutral shades, supporting a calm and professional aesthetic. Textures and organic elements are included to convey an approachable yet elegant vibe, aligning with the principles of simplicity and consistency in the name card design.

Final Name card


Brief Summary: A minimalistic and professional name card design that emphasizes easy readability and organized contact details.

Target Audience: Professionals and clients looking for clear and accessible contact information.

Colour Scheme: Warm neutrals with a focus on taupe and blush tones.

Typography: Elegant serif for the name and sans-serif for contact information.

Theme: Minimalistic, professional, and elegant.

Rationale

For the front of my name card, I chose a minimalistic design focusing on my logo "TC" and my name "Trina" This simplicity ensures a clean and professional appearance, adhering to the design principle of simplicity. The beige background contrasts nicely with the white and dark brown elements, applying the principle of contrast to make the logo and my name stand out effectively

On the back, I organized the information clearly, placing my name and title at the top, followed by my contact details and address. This clear hierarchy guides the reader’s eye smoothly through the information, utilizing the principle of hierarchy. I added a floral design on the right to bring an aesthetic touch without overwhelming the contact details, maintaining a balance between functionality and visual appeal, which follows the principle of balance. The consistent color palette on both sides creates a cohesive look, adhering to the principle of consistency
