Design Concept

There are 2 conceptual design to derive into the conclusion of the name card. The concept sketches allows the designer to gather ideas of how they want their end product to look like.

The "Trina's ChocoBoutique" name card combines earthy tones, elegant serif typography, and a playful chocolate-inspired logo to reflect the brand's richness and artisanal quality. Its clean layout and subtle details create a sophisticated yet approachable design. I chose this over the Book Cafe card because it feels more unique, aligns better with my brand’s identity, and effectively conveys a sense of indulgence and craftsmanship.

The "Trina's ChocoBoutique" name card uses AR technology with an AR robot, creating an interactive experience. When viewed through the robot's camera, it showcases dynamic brand elements like chocolate products or animations, making the card innovative, memorable, and aligned with modern branding trends.

Key Functions

  • Search Functionality: Utilizes useState to dynamically filter and display menu items based on user input, ensuring an efficient and user-friendly search experience.
  • Real-Time Shop Status: Automatically updates the shop's open or closed status based on the user's local time, providing accurate and transparent availability information.
  • React Integration: Demonstrates the use of React hooks like useState for state management, offering a practical example of integrating dynamic and real-time functionality in web applications.
