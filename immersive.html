OVERVIEW OF THE ESCAPE ROOM

This document specifies a design VR gameplay of a game titled “The Elemental Crystal Conservatory”. It was well thought and planned on 29th November 2021. The architecture of the game was greatly inspired by puzzle game called The Room Series and design and ideas from the game called Fire boy & water girl elements Thus, the project was created by Trina chua.

STORY

Players find themselves trapped within a mystical conservatory where the balance among four elemental crystals (Earth, Water, Crystal, and Fire) has been disrupted. This imbalance has locked the conservatory's doors, and to escape, players must restore harmony to each element by solving puzzles that align with their respective characteristics.

Game Puzzles

1. Earth Crystal Puzzle

The objective of the puzzle is to break wooden crates using an axe and collect the stone items hidden within. These stones must then be placed on the stone table, triggering the appearance of a pickaxe. The pickaxe is used to unlock the door, allowing progression to the next puzzle. Players should pay attention to environmental clues to figure out the necessary steps. Interactions include using the axe to break the crates, placing the stones on the stone table, and using the pickaxe to unlock the door and advance.

2. Crystal Puzzle

The objective of the Crystal Puzzle is to arrange colored crystals onto their corresponding stone platforms on the ground. Clues are provided by the colored crystals placed beside each stone platform, guiding players to match them correctly. The interaction involves dragging and dropping the crystals onto the appropriate platforms, unlocking the next puzzle stage upon successful completion.

3. Water Crystal Puzzle

In the Water Crystal Puzzle, players must decipher a clue to determine the correct sequence of buttons to press on a stone bridge suspended over water. Once the buttons are pressed in the correct order, the bridge will appear. The final step involves finding the correct key to unlock the door to the next stage. Clues include the crystal buttons' sequence and the location of the key. Interactions require players to press buttons in the right sequence and use the key to proceed.

4. Fire Crystal Puzzle

The Fire Crystal Puzzle requires players to light candles in ascending height order to unlock the door. Clues include the varying sizes of the candles and a scroll that provides a hint about the correct sequence. Players interact by lighting the candles in the correct order, triggering the door to open and allowing progression.
